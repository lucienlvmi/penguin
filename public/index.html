Penguin Tales

Baby Penguin's Adventure Game

Welcome to Penguin Tales! Baby penguin was just born today.

Help him with his first baby steps while trying to avoid Mr. Polar OverBear and Mrs. Snotty Seal.
Don't forget Lord Mega Orca-don hiding in the sea and Lady Sea Lioness too!
Be warn! Our baby penguin is an eager beaver and excited to see the world and can't hardly wait to explore it.

There are perils and adventures awaiting our brave little penguin but with your guidance and patience, Baby P. will rule the world!
